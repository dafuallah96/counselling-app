About us
Who we are? We are here to help out the cyberbully victims to share their stories and get advice from experts to overcome their situation. This idea was initiated because of censorious situation which is increase in number of cyberbully victims in Malaysia and globally. Due to cyberbully, there are many suicidal cases and depression where the victims does not have a platform to share their stories in order to get help to cope with cyberbully from the experts such as counsellor and psychiatrist. Users/ Victims can seek help from our professional counselors to help them in order to overcome their problems of being the victim of cyberbullying.
Our counselors are trained and qualified to be able to give them some useful tips in order to overcome victims problems. Besides, the user can also share their experience of being cyberbullied and how they managed to overcome the situation for the other users to take example from. Together we help the needed to survive in this cyber world !
